From Participants of a Youth Week Celebration in a Church in Ghana

This page provides a concise summary of insights collected from church youth who participated in the recent Youth Week celebration.

Overview

The Youth Week celebration received very positive feedback, with no negative responses recorded. Participants were engaged throughout the week, from the first day to the climax of the event.

Key Insights

  • Faith Engagement: 60% of the youth expressed interest in deepening their faith with God, showing that the main aim‚Äîto draw participants closer to their Creator‚Äîwas successfully achieved.
  • Activity Preferences: 35% of suggestions recommended including more social/recreational activities, such as game nights, excursions, and movie nights, for future Youth Week celebrations.
  • Event Popularity: The Climax and Dinner was the most popular event, while Games & Networking and the Health Talk attracted less interest.
  • Overall Satisfaction: Among participants who provided feedback, the celebration received universal acclaim, with ratings ranging from 4 to 5 out of 5.

Recommendations

  • Maintain events that foster spiritual engagement.
  • Introduce more social and recreational activities to balance the schedule.
  • Adjust the lineup of less popular events, focusing resources on those that generate excitement and participation.
