About Me

Turning Ideas Into Reality

I am Courage Kofi Hormenu, a tech-driven problem-solver with a passion for creating solutions that are functional, insightful, and user-focused. My growth in the world of technology has been shaped by curiosity, consistency, and a genuine desire to understand how things work—from building intuitive interfaces to analyzing complex datasets and transforming raw information into meaningful insights.

My journey into web development began with a fascination for how interactive experiences are crafted on the internet. Over time, I strengthened my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design by working on personal projects and refining layouts until they felt natural, clean, and impactful. Today, I enjoy building websites that balance aesthetics with functionality.

Beyond web development, I have built a strong foundation in data analytics, using Python, Power BI, and real-world datasets to uncover patterns, solve practical problems, and drive smarter decision-making. I’ve also developed an interest in Artificial Intelligence and have completed courses in Modern AI and Generative AI, allowing me to understand how new technologies shape the future of problem-solving.

Youth Survey Form Debugging & Data Handling

Fixed a broken data-submission flow on a youth survey form by restructuring the JavaScript, implementing Netlify form handling, and ensuring data was submitted correctly without page reloads. Improved reliability and user experience.
