About Me

Turning Ideas Into Reality

I am Courage Kofi Hormenu, a problem-solver who believes in building things that are both functional and meaningful. My journey into tech did not happen overnight. It grew from curiosity, long nights experimenting with code, fixing broken layouts, analyzing messy datasets, and discovering how much impact a simple, well-structured solution can make.

My journey into web development began with a curiosity for how websites are built and a desire to bring ideas to life on the web. Over the years, I have honed my skills through various projects, both personal and professional, and have developed a keen eye for design and detail.
